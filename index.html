About Me

I am Rabee Luqmann, a passionate author who crafts stories that touch the deepest corners of the human heart. My journey in literature is fueled by a desire to explore love, longing, and the complex tapestry of human emotions across diverse cultures.

Each book is a new adventure, a new exploration of what makes us human. From the traditional landscapes of India in "Bound by Destiny" to the raw emotional depths in "Endorphin," my writing invites readers on journeys they won't soon forget.
